Who Are We?

The National Association for Early Intervention (NAFEI) is a purpose-driven community of educators, specialists, families, and advocates dedicated to one mission: giving children with diverse abilities the very best start in life.

Our Mission

To uplift educators, families, and professionals with transformative tools, knowledge, and a shared vision for inclusive early childhood success.

Our Vision

A world where no child is left behind—where inclusivity is the norm, and children of all abilities are surrounded by understanding, opportunity, and support.

Our Core Values

We live by our name: NAFEI represents our commitment to creating inclusive, equitable, and inspiring early intervention services.

N
Nurturing
We create compassionate, responsive environments where children thrive and feel supported at every stage of their development.
A
Accessibility
We eliminate barriers so every child can access the care and support they need, regardless of background or circumstances.
E
Equity
We commit to justice and fairness in every aspect of early intervention, ensuring all children have equal opportunities.
I
Inspiration
We fuel hope, action, and innovation to empower families and professionals to create meaningful change.
