Supporting Early Childhood Education through collaboration and shared mission

About Our Partnership

EIEI has proudly been awarded a 5-year grant in partnership with Pennsylvania's Pre-K Counts initiative. This significant partnership allows us to support 20+ Early Childhood Education (ECE) programs throughout the region with comprehensive training, coaching, and professional development.

Through this collaborative effort, we work together with ECE programs to strengthen inclusive practices, enhance classroom management, engage families, and support the growth and development of children with diverse abilities.
