Supporting Inclusions Through Programs & Affiliates

Building collaborative partnerships to advance inclusive education and support children with diverse abilities

Our Partnership Philosophy

We join hands with non-profit and for-profit partners, aligning our efforts to support their mission through essential resources, empowering education, and collaborative efforts. By working together with community organizations, we create a stronger network of support for children and families.

EIEI believes in the power of collaboration to create systemic change. Through strategic partnerships with organizations that share our commitment to inclusion, we can amplify our impact and reach more children and families in our community.

How We Support Partners

Strategic Partnership Development
Work together to identify shared goals and develop meaningful collaborative initiatives.
Financial Resources & Grants
Access to funding opportunities and resources to support program growth and sustainability.
Staff Training & Capacity Building
Professional development for your team to strengthen inclusive practices and special education expertise.
Program Evaluation & Improvement
Data-driven assessment and guidance for continuous program improvement and quality enhancement.
Collaborative Community Initiatives
Joint programs and projects that strengthen the entire early childhood ecosystem.
Advocacy & Support
Partner with us to advocate for inclusive policies and support families in our community.

Our Partnership Approach

🤝 Collaborative Model We listen to your organization's unique needs and work together to develop customized solutions that align with your mission and goals.
🎯 Mission-Driven We partner with organizations committed to serving children with diverse abilities and fostering inclusive communities.
💪 Strength in Numbers Together, we create a stronger network that can accomplish more than we could individually, benefiting more children and families.
📈 Sustainability We help ensure your organization's long-term success through resource sharing, capacity building, and collaborative funding efforts.

Types of Organizations We Partner With

  • Early Childhood Education (ECE) programs and pre-K centers
  • Non-profit social service organizations
  • School districts and educational institutions
  • Community health and wellness organizations
  • Family support and advocacy organizations
  • For-profit businesses committed to community impact
  • Government agencies serving children and families
